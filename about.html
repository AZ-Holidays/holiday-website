About

Holidays, planned just for you.

We have been planning holidays for over 20 years, qualified independent travel agents. What started as planning trips for friends and family has grown by word of mouth into a small, careful business with clients across the UK.

The premise is simple: every itinerary is built from first principles around the person taking it. There's no template, no preferred-partner list to push, no upselling. If a hotel isn't right for you, it doesn't go in the brief.

See drone footage and on-the-ground photography from places we've personally visited and recommend. It started as a personal hobby — checking that places actually look like the brochure. It turned out clients liked seeing it too.
